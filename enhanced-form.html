Top 5 Benefits of HTML5

  1. Improved semantics for better SEO and accessibility
  2. Native support for audio and video without plugins
  3. Enhanced form controls with built-in validation
  4. Offline capabilities with local storage
  5. Responsive and mobile-friendly design elements
